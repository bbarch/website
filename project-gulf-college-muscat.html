Gulf College,
Muscat.

Muscat · Sultanate of Oman

bbArch is developing the master-plan, landscape and design of Gulf College in Muscat, Oman — a project of over 50,000 sq.m. of built space with 15 acres of landscape.

The brief includes a mosque, a student activity centre, sports facilities and residential housing for faculty and students. The project is at the design-development phase.
