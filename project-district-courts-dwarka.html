District Courts,
Dwarka.

Dwarka · New Delhi

This project for the High Court of Delhi, executed through the Delhi PWD, is a landmark in Dwarka — a 75,000 sq.m. building completed in 2008 at a cost of around Rs. 200 Crores.

For the first time, we proposed a vertical circulation system separating the movement of judges and the public — readily accepted by the Hon'ble High Court and now recommended for the district courts built since. Our scope included master-planning, architecture, landscape, interiors and project supervision.
