Secure sign-in

bbArch Access.

bbArch Access is the secure sign-in application operated by Babbar & Babbar Architects (“bbArch”). It lets our employees, consultants, contractors and clients authenticate themselves in order to reach protected bbArch online resources — including document portals, project files and internal applications.

What bbArch Access does

bbArch Access provides authentication only. When you sign in, it confirms your identity through Google Sign-In and, once verified, authorises your access to the appropriate bbArch resources. Access to those resources is further protected by bbArch’s Zero Trust access controls. The application does not publish content of its own; its sole purpose is to verify who you are before granting access.

What information it uses

bbArch Access uses only the basic account information required to sign you in — your name and email address. It does not store, sell, or share your Google account data, and it does not use that data for advertising or for any purpose other than authenticating you and authorising your access to bbArch resources.

bbArch’s use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Who it is for

bbArch Access is intended for people bbArch has authorised — our staff, project consultants, contractors and clients. If you have reached this page and believe you should have access but do not, please contact us at info@bbarch.net.

More information

For details on how we handle your information, see our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service. bbArch Access is operated by Babbar & Babbar Architects, New Delhi, India.
