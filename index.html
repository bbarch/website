Who we are

A multidisciplinary practice that treats design as an action-oriented problem solving process.

Babbar & Babbar Architects was founded on the conviction that innovative architectural and urban design demand a multidisciplinary approach to their solution. Every project we undertake is led directly by at least one of the partners.

We believe that for urban development to be compatible with human need and to preserve our resources, ecological guidelines must be followed — the conservation of energy, of manpower, and of our heritage. Alternatives at every level, arrived at after constant and deliberate study, is the firm's ideology.

M/s Babbar & Babbar Architects has a history of over 40 years, with projects of varying magnitude executed in India and internationally. bbArch is headquartered in New Delhi.

The people

The principals

Each partner brings a distinct technical background and body of experience. Together they lead every project the firm undertakes.

Ameet Babbar

Managing Partner · Architect & Landscape Architect

Educational qualifications

Dip.Arch. (New Delhi); M.L.Arch. (SPA, New Delhi); Fellow, Indian Institute of Architects; Fellow, Indian Society of Landscape Architects.

Professional background

Over 28 years of experience across architecture and landscape design. For the past decade his responsibilities have included design, design development and the role of studio head. He has regularly been visiting faculty at the School of Planning and Architecture, New Delhi, as well as other academic programmes.

Anupama Babbar Singh

Partner · Architect & General Manager

Educational qualifications

B.Arch.; MBA (Delhi School of Economics).

Professional background

Over 30 years of varied experience across all aspects of design and project management. She heads corporate design and process management for bbArch as well as the firm's international projects.

Baldev Babbar

Founder · Architect & Town Planner

Educational qualifications

B.Arch. (SPA, New Delhi); PG Dip. T.P. (Leeds, UK); Fellow, Indian Institute of Architects.

Professional background

Over 40 years of professional experience across many fields of architecture and planning, including five years in England working on varied redevelopment projects. For the past 25 years his responsibilities have spanned design, project management and administration of the firm's projects in India and abroad.

Santosh Auluck

Sr. Consultant · Architect & Planner

Educational qualifications

B.Arch. (SPA, New Delhi); Fellow, Indian Institute of Architects.

Professional background

Over 35 years of professional experience across England, the USA, Chandigarh and Delhi. He retired as Chief Architect of the Delhi Development Authority in 1996 and now heads contracts, planning regulations and permitting processes at the office.
