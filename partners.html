The people

The principals.

Each partner brings a distinct technical background and body of experience. Together they lead every project the firm undertakes.

Ameet Babbar

Managing Partner · Architect & Landscape Architect

Educational qualifications

Dip.Arch. (New Delhi); Postgraduate Landscape Architect, M.L.Arch. (SPA, New Delhi); Fellow, Indian Institute of Architects; Fellow, Indian Society of Landscape Architects; International Associate, American Institute of Architects.

Professional background

Over 25 years of experience across architecture and landscape design. His responsibilities span design, design development and the role of studio head. He has regularly been visiting faculty at the School of Planning and Architecture, New Delhi, as well as other academic programmes.

Anupama Babbar Singh

Partner · Architect & MBA (HR)

Educational qualifications

B.Arch.; MBA / Postgraduate (Delhi School of Economics); Registered, Council of Architecture; Associate, Indian Institute of Architects.

Professional background

Over 29 years of varied experience across all aspects of design and project management. She heads corporate design and process management for bbArch as well as the firm's international projects.

Baldev Babbar

Founder Partner · Architect & Town Planner (retired 2021)

Educational qualifications

B.Arch. (SPA, New Delhi); Master of Town Planning (Leeds, UK); Fellow, Indian Institute of Architects.

Professional background

Over 40 years of professional experience across many fields of architecture and planning, including five years in England working on varied redevelopment projects. His responsibilities spanned design, project management and administration of the firm's projects in India and abroad. He retired from bbArch in 2021.

Santosh Auluck

Sr. Consultant · Architect & Planner (retired 2021)

Educational qualifications

B.Arch. (SPA, New Delhi); Fellow, Indian Institute of Architects.

Professional background

Over 35 years of professional experience across England, the USA, Chandigarh and Delhi. He retired as Chief Architect of the Delhi Development Authority in 1996 and headed contracts, planning regulations and permitting processes at the office. He retired from bbArch in 2021.
